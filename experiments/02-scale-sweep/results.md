## Headline: hidden-intermediate visibility vs model size

`inter` = rank of the unstated intermediate concept (e.g. *Italy* in the boot→currency hop) at the **best** layer and position for it — a generous upper bound on workspace visibility. `control` = the identical search for an intermediate borrowed from a different prompt: the noise floor of the metric. The signal is the gap between the two.
